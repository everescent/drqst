Health system:

Player gets 3 lives at the start of the stage. 
Food items will add one life. 
During the tutorial stage, enemies will deal 1/2 life worth of damage. 
Basic enemies will have one life while stronger mobs have 2. 
Each bosses will have a unique amount of lives depending on the boss mechanic. 

Boss A.I:

-Merlin: 
Blink every X time. X will be relative to how many moves he can make in that time.
